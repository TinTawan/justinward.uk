Game Dev Soceity Award!

GDS Win the Best New Academic Society at the Student Led Teaching Awards

Each year at the University of Greenwich, the Student Led Teaching Awards take place. This Student-Led celebration aims to showcase the best of teaching and academic excellence, for lecturers, faculties, student support, programme reps, and societies.

In the 2024 awards, the Game Development Society was nominated for the Best New Academic Society, up against the CIB & Financial Services Society, after we brought it back from the dead for us and game devs alike to enjoy. To our great excitement and enjoyment, we went up on stage to collect our certificate and glass trophy, which the society treasure to this day.
