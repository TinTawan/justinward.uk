Kero Master: Kaeru

Kero Master: Kaeru - Group Project

GitHub

The Project

• 3D platformer collectathon created in my first year at university
• Group project - team size of 3
• You play as a frog who wants to catch flies and can grapple with his tongue to help platforming
• Switch camera perspectives to 3rd person to use the full movement when platforming, and 1st person to shoot your tongue out to catch flies and grapple

My Part

• I took the role of the lead programmer and a position of leader
• I coded the main mechanics of switching camera perspectives, using the tongue to catch flies, and using the tongue to grapple on vines, as well as the player's basic camera controls and movement
• This project was the first time I used Git, through which I learnt its importance when working in a team to avoid merge conflicts and keep communication at a high priority
