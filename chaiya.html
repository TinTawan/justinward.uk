Chaiya Charge

Chaiya Charge - A Level Computer Science NEA

The Project

• 2D infinite-scrolling platformer created for my A Level Computer Science NEA
• Randomly spawns the level as the player moves in chunks to make each run unique
• Score can be saved under a username to an external database, which is queried to display the top 5 high scores
• Paralax background that moves around the camera for optimisation, so the background doesn't need to be copied along the whole x axis

I was awarded an A* for the NEA section of my Computer Science A Level, which helped me achieve a grade A overall
