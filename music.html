Music

My Love for Music and Music for Games

Music plays a very big part in my life. I've always been into music, whether that be playing it myself or hearing others! I love to sing and mess around on guitar a bit too, and I'd love to try being in a band at some point - maybe a future goal.
Concerts and gigs are some of my favourite things to go to, especially small rock gigs with bands most people won't have heard of. My favourite band, Bad Nerves, are definitly my favourite to see, as I've met them multiple times and love talking to them at their shows!
Hearing all the amazing music at gigs inspires me to make my own, so for my game Temporus, I want to make all the music and sound effects myself! You can listen to the main menu music below. I wanted to make it sound eerie and echoey as if the player is inside the mansion the game is set in, with the sounds engulfing them before they press start. The background music for Tempral Bane, currently work in progress, is a breakcore beat in a creepy, gothic style, while being fast paced to show the fast movement of the player in the mansion.
Below is also the loop tracks I made for my OOP Project, Dauntless Depths! Both songs are played as background music for the menu area and level, and have fairly up-beat feel to them to show the fun aspects of the game, while using bass to make the user feel as if they are deep in the dungeon.
