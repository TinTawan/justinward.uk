About Me


Hi I'm Justin! Thanks for checking out my portfolio :)

I'm a final year student at the University of Greenwich studying BSc Games Design and Development at a first class level. As someone on the programming pathway of the course, I'm looking to specialise in coding on my journey to becoming a true game developer. In group projects, my role is usually the core mechanics programmer and creating the main interactions in games, but in personal projects I love to put my all into my games, using all the knowledge I've gained from my degree and taking part in game jams.

I'm well experienced in C# and Unity, with 5 years under my belt, and in being a Unity tutor to secondary school children taking part in the DofE after receiving my own Gold DofE award at Buckingham Palace in 2023. My game dev knowledge extends to Unreal Engine and C++, as well as shader programming in GLSL and HLSL with implementation in UE5. My work during my degree is all First Class level, showing the effort and hard work I put in to each module - I'm known for my resilience and strong work ethic! Thanks to this, I've been nominated for the Outstanding TIGA Graduate of the Year: Programmer at the TIGA UK Games Education Awards 2025!

While I've been at UoG, I've been happy to be a part of multiple societies: Game Development Society, Badminton club, Snowsports, and Climbing. I'm very proud to have been a committee member of the new Game Dev Society at the Uni of Greenwich last year where we won the Best New Academic Society Award at the Student Led Teaching Awards! Check us out on Instagram or LinkedIn! I've been part of the Badminton club for 3 years and can't wait to keep playing even after I graduate - I've found a sport I love to play thanks to UoG. I joined the Snowsports society in my final year where I joined them on the annual ski trip! We went to Les Deux Alpes in France where I learnt how to snowboard - seriously one of the coolest trips I've been on and I hope I can keep snowboarding in the future.
