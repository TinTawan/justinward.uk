TIGA UK Games Education Awards 2024

So You're Tellin' Me...? - Finalist for the Best Student Game 2024!

TIGA, the trade association representing the UK games industry, hold awards for both industry and education each year. A ceremony for educational learners, graduates, teachers, researchers and universities showcases some of the best games and courses across the country and offers the chance for those to shine. As the University of Greenwich games course is now TIGA Acredited, we are give the opportunity to be nominated for these awards, with 14 total accolades in total.

My 2nd year project So You're Tellin' Me...? was nominated for the Creative Assembly: Best Student Game 2024 award, where we reached the finals, going up against 12 other games from around the country and had our game showcased in the livestreamed ceremony. Unfortunately, we didn't win the award, but myself and my two team mates are incredibly proud of SYTM and reaching the finals! Here's to next year's awards!

The University also had nominations for our games course, where we won the Diversity Award!
